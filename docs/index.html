How it works

Three steps. Thirty seconds.

  1. 1

    Open an Article

    Navigate to any X long-form Article and click the extension icon.

  2. 2

    Pick your style

    Modern or Serif, A4 or Letter, with or without a source link.

  3. 3

    Export

    Save as PDF through the print dialog, or download Markdown instantly.

Under the hood it walks X's Draft.js article tree into a neutral list of segments, keeps an allowlist of inline tags, and renders into a CSP-safe page it fully controls. Selectors live in one file, so an X redesign is a small patch — contributions welcome.

Install in one click

Prefer to load it unpacked? (for development)
  1. Download the repo (clone or ZIP) and unzip it.
  2. Open chrome://extensions.
  3. Turn on Developer mode (top-right).
  4. Click Load unpacked and choose the folder.
  5. Pin the icon and open any X Article.
